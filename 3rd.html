Advanced Nutrition & Superfoods

Your baby is growing fast, so focus on **high-protein and energy-boosting foods**:

  • Eggs & Dairy: High in **protein** and calcium for strong bones.
  • Oats & Whole Grains: Help maintain **stable energy levels**.
  • Spinach & Red Meat: Rich in **iron** to prevent anemia.
  • Dates: May help **prepare the cervix for labor**.
  • Water & Coconut Water: Essential for **hydration**.

Safe Exercise & Labor Preparation

Gentle movements **reduce swelling, improve circulation, and prepare your body for labor**:

  • Walking: Helps baby move into position.
  • Prenatal Squats: Strengthens pelvic muscles for birth.
  • Deep Breathing & Meditation: Reduces stress and prepares for labor.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

When should I go to the hospital?

If contractions are **5 minutes apart, lasting 1 minute, for 1 hour** ("5-1-1 Rule").

What should I pack for the hospital?

Essentials: **ID, baby clothes, snacks, phone charger, and birth plan.**
