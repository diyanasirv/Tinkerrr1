Advanced Nutrition & Superfoods

As your baby grows, you need more **calories and essential nutrients**:

  • Lean Meat & Lentils: Rich in **iron** to prevent anemia.
  • Bananas & Whole Grains: High in **fiber**, reducing constipation.
  • Oranges & Strawberries: Packed with **Vitamin C** for better immunity.
  • Nuts & Seeds: Provide **healthy fats** and proteins.
  • Dark Chocolate: Helps regulate **blood pressure**.

Safe Exercise & Yoga

Maintaining an active lifestyle helps **reduce back pain and swelling**. Recommended exercises include:

  • Swimming: Relieves joint pressure.
  • Prenatal Yoga: Strengthens pelvic muscles.
  • Light Strength Training: Improves posture and stamina.

Mental Health & Emotional Well-being

The second trimester is when many women feel more emotionally stable, but anxiety can still occur:

  • Practice **positive affirmations** about your pregnancy journey.
  • Engage in **hobbies or meditation** for relaxation.
  • Stay connected with **friends, family, or support groups**.
